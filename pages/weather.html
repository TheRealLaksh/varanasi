Best Time to Visit

Plan your perfect trip to Varanasi

❄️

Winter (Oct - Mar)

10°C to 25°C

Winter's cool, pleasant weather makes exploring Varanasi's ghats, temples, and markets enjoyable. The season also hosts vibrant festivals like Diwali, Dev Deepawali, and Maha Shivratri, enhancing the city's spiritual and cultural allure. Experiencing a sunrise boat ride or Ganga Aarti is particularly enchanting during this time

✅ Highly Recommended
☀️

Summer (Apr - Jun)

30°C to 45°C

In summer, visiting Varanasi can be challenging due to the oppressive heat, which makes outdoor exploration and sightseeing uncomfortable. Strolling along the ghats or experiencing boat rides can be difficult under the scorching sun. Many attractions are less enjoyable, and spending time indoors might become necessary. While there might be fewer crowds, the severe conditions can impact the overall experience

⚠️ Avoid if possible
🌧️

Monsoon (Jul - Sep)

25°C to 33°C

Monsoon in Varanasi presents a mixed experience. While the lush greenery and cooler air offer a respite from summer's heat, heavy rainfall can disrupt travel plans and sightseeing. Activities like boat rides might be suspended due to high river levels. However, the spiritual ambiance is heightened, and there are fewer crowds

🌦️ Moderate choice
