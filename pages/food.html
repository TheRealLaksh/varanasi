Banarasi Cuisine

Taste the authentic flavors of Varanasi

Breakfast

Kachori Sabzi

Crispy deep-fried pastries with spiced lentil filling served with potato curry

Where to try: Street vendors, Deena Chat Bhandar
Snack

Banarasi Chaat

Mix of crispy puris, potatoes, chickpeas with tangy chutneys and spices

Where to try: Chaat stalls, Godowlia Market
Beverage

Banarasi Lassi

Creamy yogurt drink flavored with cardamom and topped with nuts

Where to try: Lassi shops near ghats
After meal

Banarasi Paan

Betel leaf wrap with areca nut, lime, and various spices

Where to try: Paan shops in Vishwanath Lane
Main course

Baati Chokha

Roasted wheat balls served with spicy mashed vegetables

Where to try: Local eateries, street vendors
Dessert

Malaiyo

Fluffy, creamy dessert made from milk and flavored with cardamom

Where to try: Local eateries, street vendors
